Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a property?

To book a property, simply browse our listings, select your desired dates, and click "Book Now". You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your reservation.

2. What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies that vary by property. Each listing clearly displays its specific cancellation policy before you book.

3. How do I contact the property owner?

After booking, you'll have access to our secure messaging system to communicate directly with the property owner or manager.

4. What payment methods are accepted?

After booking, you'll have access to our secure psyment system tat accepts M-Pesa mode of payment.

5. Are there additional fees after I have booked my stay?

After booking, Some hosts may charge a cleaning fee or security deposit.

6. . Do I need a visa to stay in an Airbnb in Kenya?

Foreign visitors may need a Kenyan eVisa (check requirements based on nationality) but East African citizens (Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, etc.) may not need a visa.
